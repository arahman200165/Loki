Why Loki

Private by design, not buried in settings.

Deliberate onboarding

Create an account without tying your identity to a phone number or email address.

Intentional contact flow

People connect through exact Public-IDs and recipient approval, not global search or typeahead.

Minimal retention

Encrypted delivery stays short-lived, with disappearing messages and stronger local privacy controls on the device.

Built for the first release

Focused enough to ship. Clear enough to trust.

Loki is not trying to be a bloated everything-app. The first version centers on private onboarding, encrypted messaging, group chat, calling, and device-level protection that feels credible in daily use.
